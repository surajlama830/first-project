Service Area


The organization is providing efficient micro credit services to poor people in the rural areas of Sindhupalchok, Rasuwa & Nuwakot where banking activities are in short supply.NSLBSL has now rendering services to 12 VDC's of Sindhupalchok district which being gradually increasing its territory of this district. It is providing micro financial services to around 50 thousand poor people & planning to extend towards other remaining VDC's of Sindhupalchok with around 50 thousand poor people within the fiscal year 2072/73. The company is planning to extend soon its branches at Rasuwa and Nuwakot districts.


Service Area Expansion

First AGM held on 2072/9/29 approved to extend its service area to 10 districts. It is going to add seven more districts area for its service territory on obtaining permission from Nepal Rastra Bank. i.e Kavre, Ramechham, Sindhuli, Makawanapur, Bara, Chitwan & Dhading.
