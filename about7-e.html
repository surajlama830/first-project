Mr. Durga Prasad Malla - Member


Mr. Malla was recently appointed as a director from second AGM held on 2073/12/4. He is renowned business tycoon in the field of construction business. Currently he is engaged as a Managing Director of Bhaktapur Brick & Tile Factory.


As a social mobilizer he has been engaged in Reukai, Nepal Red Cross Society, and Family Planning Association of Nepal (FPAN). Mr. Dangol has also engaged in Cooperative organizations and has experience as Program Coordinator in the PAF project of Community Development Center, Nepal.
