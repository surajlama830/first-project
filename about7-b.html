Mr. Udayaswor Shrestha - Member


Professionally as an auditor for decades, Mr. Udayaswor Shrestha, an MBA, is a gentle person. He is also associated with community developments activities for a long period. Mr. Shrestha, a member of Institute of Chartered Accountant of Nepal (ICAN) has exentsive auditing experience in of bank and financialinstitution. He is also rendering consultancy services to a number of organisation.


Mr. Shrestha visited European countries of Germany, Poland, the Netherlands, Japan,China, Pakistan, Bangaladesh and India and neighboring countries to attend different programs and personal trips. Mr. Shrestha has extensively travelled in the country to 73 districts out of 75 districts of Nepal.
